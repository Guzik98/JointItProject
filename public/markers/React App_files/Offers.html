Description
Finiata was founded in 2016 and is funded by top European investors. Our team is filled with curious people who want to learn while having fun and consists of more than 60 talents from 19 nations located across offices in Germany, Poland, and Ukraine. We appreciate the power of diversity and share a passion for product development and creating exceptional user experiences that have a real impact on our customers lives. Join a fast-growing company of smart and ambitious people who are on a mission to support small businesses to master their liquidity with ease.

YOUR MISSION

  • Own product delivery with a cross-functional team to provide top-flight UX while meeting and/or exceeding our operational KPI targets
  • Drive the roadmap backlog of our credit decisioning experience and its underlying technology, including our infrastructure, APIs, related data services, pipelines, machine learning models and proprietary technology
  • Champion data governance practices across the organization, pushing data quality to the next level while aligning stakeholders to take credit-related business decisions in a data driven fashion
  • Be customer-centered by driving Product discovery to build the right stuff
  • Prioritise ruthlessly with top-notch communication, guaranteeing the teams focus and scalable value delivery

YOUR PROFILE

  • 3+ years of experience as a Product Manager or a Technical Lead on a Data Engineering environment
  • Experience having worked on data intensive and/or embedded Machine Learning products
  • Experience in leading a cross-functional team using agile Scrum processes 
  • Proven track record with Data Platforms and Data Engineering projects with knowledge of common infrastructure components and solutions
  • Top-flight biz2dev communication and vice versa
  • An empathic person with a bridge-building mindset
  • Able to make informed decisions based on qualitative user insights and quantitative user data by conducting research, A/B tests, and analyzing operations data
  • Fluent in English

Nice-to-Haves:
  • Start-up / scale-up experience
  • Polish or German proficiency
  • Exposure to OKR-based strategy canvas
  • Experience on driving data products within AWS cloud platform

WHY US?

  • Get to make an impact on thousands of businesses every day
  • Participate in a self-organized, continuously improving team
  • Work with the best and learn from other competent and brilliant people
  • Be supported to grow and take leadership from day one
  • Get insights and learnings about the financial business and technology
  • Benefit from remote work opportunities, flexible working hours and a Company Holiday on 24th and 31st December
  • Benefit from an office in the centre of Warsaw with great perks (e.g. good coffee, fresh fruit, Multisport membership)
  • Have the option to take German language courses
  • Have the option to bring your best buddy to work. We love dogs!
  • Be paid a competitive salary
